Intergalactic Paws

Project Mission

Intergalactic Paws is a demolition derby in space. The main character is a humaniod cat who is defending Earth from aliens that have the intent to invade our home planet. Every 100 years, all of the races in the galaxy come together to battle it out in an aggressive championship on the moon. The player must stay in the crater of the moon and must keep their car running, in order to win. If you are knocked out of the arena, or blow your engine, then you lose and that is the downfall of Earth as we know it.

Mechanics

Must Haves:

Gravity
- The gravity is similar to earth inside
Player Controls
- WASD to move and space to shoot
- Player can ram enemies or shoot them to damage
Projectiles
- The player can shoot the enemies to damage them instead of hitting them with your own vehicle
Timer
- Match tiems last 3 minutes, if the player survives the timer, they win the match. If the player dies before the timer is up they lose
